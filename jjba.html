The Honorable Bruno Bucciarati

"To complete my mission and protect my men. Having to do both at once is what makes being a capo so tough. Are you ready for this? I know I am."

Bucciarati's Honored Life Events:
  • Bucciarati takes his gang to the island of Capri in order to seize four billion lire worth of hidden treasure that once belonged to Polpo. After a major ambush, the enemy takes down the majority of their gang and Bruno is left alone to defeat the enemy.
  • Bucciarati meets with the ringleader, Pericolo, after completing his mission. After a quick examination, Pericolo accepts and promotes Bucciarati to the rank of capo, giving him full control of the hotels and casino of Naples.
  • While protecting the capo's daughter Trish, Bucciarati encounters more enemies attacking his gang. To no surprise, he risks and almost loses his life during the battle.
  • After a long journey, Bucciarati is successful in protecting the capo's daughter. He meets with the capo, Diavolo, to bring him Trish, only to be faced with his final battle. Bucciarati is shocked as Diavolo attacks Trish and in an attempt to save her, Bucciarati dies an honorable death.
